Our Specialties

Authentic Jamaican Baked Goods

Jamaican Patties

Flaky, golden pastries filled with seasoned spiced beef. Crispy on the outside, tender on the inside. A true taste of Jamaica in every bite.

Coco Bread

Soft, pillowy bread with a touch of coconut sweetness. Perfect for breakfast or as a standalone snack. Made fresh daily using Granny's time-honored recipe.

Bun and Cheese

The perfect marriage of sweet and savory. Soft, slightly sweet bun paired with sharp cheddar cheese. A Jamaican classic reimagined with love.

Hardoe Bread

Dense, hearty bread with deep Caribbean flavors. Packed with nutrition and tradition. Perfect alongside any meal or enjoyed on its own.
